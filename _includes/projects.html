Torq Interface

Torq is an app for medical device representatives. Joint replacement surgeries require a human from the company which created the joint replacement to be in the operating room with the surgeon. As the surgeon's schedule changes throughout the day, reps get paid to wait around or hurry to the hospital. Torq sends reps live schedule updates using push notifications by hooking into hospitals' electronic health records. I scaled Torq from zero lines of code to its use by over 20 hospitals around Boston.
